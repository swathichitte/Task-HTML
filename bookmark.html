About

10000Coders is a premier EdTech platform committed to transforming the lives of aspiring software developers. Our mission is to empower individuals through industry-relevant training in cutting-edge technologies such as Node.js, Python, React Native, and AWS. With a blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on projects, we prepare our students to excel in the competitive software industry. Our dedicated placement support ensures students secure rewarding opportunities, making their career aspirations a reality.

Features

  • Expert-Led Courses: Learn from industry professionals who have been there, done that.
  • Hands-On Projects: Build real-world projects that employers will notice.
  • AI-Powered Support: Get assistance with coding challenges and course-related queries.
  • Mentorship: Receive personalized guidance and feedback from experienced mentors.
  • Community: Connect with like-minded individuals, share ideas, and grow together.
